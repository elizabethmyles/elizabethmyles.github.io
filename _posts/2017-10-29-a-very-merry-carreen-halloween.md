### A [*The Real You*][tru] Bonus Holiday Special

It's Halloween time in Carreen, Texas! Rourke is enjoying the annual costume party at Roy's Video when suddenly she, Dallas, and her other friends each receive a mysterious phone message.
The text invites them to come join another party happening across town, one being hosted at a "genuine haunted house" near the cemetery...

#### More About the Book

- A New Adult romance novella, approximately 33,000 words in length
- Told in third person from both Rourke's and Dallas's POV
- Includes an excerpt from [*A Kiss at Halloween: A Hillingham Hollow Romance*][akah]
- Contains PG-13 content (some crude humor, mild swearing, and closed door bedroom scenes)
{: .heart}

#### Associated Titles

- This story takes place a little over 2 years after the events in [*The Real You*][tru], and is intended for readers interested in reading more about that book's main characters.
